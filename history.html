The history of The Providentz

The ship was owned by a wealthy Cork businessman named Joseph Lavit. His family had made their fortune through a number of ventures, including whiskey production, privateering, and as merchants. The Lavits were also well-placed politically, with members of the family serving as Lord Mayors and Aldermen of the city of Cork.
